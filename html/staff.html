Our Staff

Dr. Aria Zorath – Archaeologist

Specializes in recovering alien artifacts from planets with extinct civilizations. Has led expeditions to the Zeta-5 and Orion sectors, discovering ancient alien tools and inscriptions.

Dr. Taro Venn – Anthropologist

Studies extinct extraterrestrial species and their cultures through skeletal remains and preserved environments. Focuses on social structures, communication methods, and planetary adaptation.

Professor Lyra Quen – Culturalist / Interpreter

Interprets symbolic and cultural meaning of alien artifacts using comparative analysis and AI-assisted ethnography. Special interest in rituals, myths, and cosmological beliefs of extinct civilizations.
